Car Suspension 3D Model

The following model was created based on a Car Suspension Design. The Suspension consisted of 15 different parts that are assembled. The solidworks model is a replicated scale of the actual Car Suspension. To accomplish this, the Suspension was, first, dismantled into individual parts. Then, accurate measurements using a manual caliper. The measurements were transferred into hand sketches, solidworks parts, and finally into a solidworks assembly.
